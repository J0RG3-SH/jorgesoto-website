About

Digital designer with a critical eye and a craftsman's vocation

I've spent over two decades designing digital experiences for industry-leading companies. My work spans UX consulting, interface design, and critical reflection on technology's impact on people.

Based in Asturias, I combine professional practice with writing and analysis of the tech sector, with a particular focus on artificial intelligence and its impact on design and creativity.

I believe good design is invisible: it reduces friction, respects the user, and solves real problems. At a time when AI is transforming the industry, I maintain an independent perspective on what it means to design with intention.
